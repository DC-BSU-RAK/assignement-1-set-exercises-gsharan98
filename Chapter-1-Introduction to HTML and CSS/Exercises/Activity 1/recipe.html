🍓 Strawberry Lassi

A sweet, creamy youghert based drink with blended starberries. 💖

✨ Introduction

A blend of frozen strawberries 🍓, a little sugar, and a hint of cardamom creates strawberry Lassi, a cool, creamy yogurt-based beverage. It is the ideal combination of fruity, acidic, and sweet—like a smoothie, but with a lovely Indian twist—and is perfectly chilled.

🛒Ingredients

  • 🍓 1 cup of frozen or fresh strawberries 🍓
  • 🍶 1/2 cup og plain yogurt
  • 🥛 1/2 chilled cup of milk🥛
  • 🍯 1–2 tsp honey for the sweetness 🍯
  • 🌹 3 drops of rose water🌹
  • 🧊 ice cubes according to the taste.🧊
  • ☁️whipp cream and some frozen strawberries for toppings☁️
  • 🌿 Mint or pistachios for garnish (🌿

👩‍🍳 Method

  1. Blend together the milk, yogurt, sweetener, and strawberries.
  2. If you prefer a fruity twist, add rose water.
  3. After adding ice cubes ,Blend till creamy and smooth.
  4. Transfer into a glass and add some whipped cream or mint as a garnish
  5. Sip and savor your  cool strawberry lassi !

💡 A Pro Tip

For a thick, slushy-style lassi that is especially pleasant, use frozen strawberries!
