Stranger Things

The Upside Down is closer than you think...

Plot Overview

A small child vanishes into thin air in Hawkins, Indiana, a sleepy village. Friends and family discover a top-secret experiment, a mystery girl with superhuman abilities, and a portal to another planet while they look for answers. Everything will be different because of what's underneath Hawkins.

Key Information

  • Genre: Sci-Fi, Thriller, Horror
  • Created by: The Duffer Brothers
  • Premiered: 15,July 2016
  • Streaming on: Netflix
  • Seasons: 4

Cast & Creative Team

  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb McLaughlin as Lucas Sinclair
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers

Main Characters

  • Mike: devoted, courageous, and closely connected to Eleven.
  • Hopper: The big-hearted, protective, and haunted sheriff.
  • Eleven: A girl with a frightening history and psychokinetic abilities.
  • Lucas: A voice of reason, grounded and resolute.
  • Dustin: Intelligent, humorous, and insatiably curious.
