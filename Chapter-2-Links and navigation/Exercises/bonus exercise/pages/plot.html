Plot Summary

Stranger Things is set in Hawkins, Indiana in the 1980s. The story begins when young Will Byers vanishes mysteriously. As his friends search, they encounter a girl with supernatural powers—Eleven.

The group uncovers a secret government lab and the terrifying world of the Upside Down. It’s a tale of horror, mystery, and friendship that keeps you hooked.
